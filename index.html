About TradeIQ

Most first-time exporters lose time not on finding buyers, but on figuring out paperwork — the right HS code, how much duty applies, which certificate a particular buyer's country will demand. That information exists, but it's scattered across ICEGATE, DGFT and GST circulars that weren't written for a first-time exporter to skim in five minutes.

TradeIQ puts the answer in one search box. We're building out the database product by product, starting with the categories Indian exporters ship most: textiles, spices, leather, handicrafts and agro-processed goods. If your product isn't listed yet, tell us on WhatsApp — we research it and get back within 24 hours.
